GridLockTowers

GridLockTowers is a game that combines both the strategy of tower defense games and the energy of action games in a unique way.
During waves you move your units in the right spot of them to be most effective against the upcoming enemies.
In between waves you have time to sort out your ideal layout and can buy various upgrades and units.
